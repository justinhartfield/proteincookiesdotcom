PROTEIN COOKIES FOR KIDS

Kid-approved protein cookies with 12g protein and no artificial sweeteners. Perfect for lunchboxes and after-school snacks.

INGREDIENTS

  • 150g oat flour
  • 45g vanilla whey protein powder
  • 3g baking soda
  • 1g salt
  • 170g mashed ripe banana
  • 60g honey
  • 30g coconut oil, melted
  • 5g vanilla extract
  • 45g mini chocolate chips

NUTRITION (PER 1 COOKIE)

Calories 130 kcal
Protein 12g
Carbs 16g
Fat 4g
Fiber 2g
Sugar 7g

INSTRUCTIONS

1

Preheat

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2

Mix Dry

Whisk together oat flour, protein powder, baking soda, and salt.

3

Mix Wet

Mash banana until smooth. Add honey, coconut oil, and vanilla. Mix well.

4

Combine

Add wet to dry and mix until combined. Fold in chocolate chips.

5

Shape

Scoop 16 small portions onto baking sheet.

6

Bake

Bake for 9-10 minutes until edges are golden.

7

Cool

Cool completely before packing in lunchboxes.

PRO TIPS

  • Use a kitchen scale for precise gram measurements to ensure accurate macros.
  • Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days, or freeze for up to 3 months.
  • Don't overbake - cookies will continue to firm up as they cool on the baking sheet.
