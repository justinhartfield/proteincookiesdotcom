THIN & CRISPY PROTEIN COOKIES

Perfectly thin and crispy protein cookies with 15g protein. Satisfying crunch with every bite for those who prefer crispy over chewy.

INGREDIENTS

  • 120g oat flour
  • 45g vanilla whey protein powder
  • 3g baking soda
  • 2g salt
  • 60g coconut oil, melted
  • 80g granulated sweetener
  • 1 large egg
  • 5g vanilla extract
  • 45g chocolate chips

INSTRUCTIONS

1

Preheat

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2

Mix Dry

Whisk together oat flour, protein powder, baking soda, and salt.

3

Mix Wet

Combine melted coconut oil, sweetener, egg, and vanilla until smooth.

4

Combine

Add wet to dry and mix. Fold in chocolate chips.

5

Shape

Scoop 16 small portions onto baking sheet, spacing 3 inches apart. Flatten very thin.

6

Bake

Bake for 12-14 minutes until golden brown throughout.

7

Cool

Cool on baking sheet completely. Cookies will crisp up as they cool.
