CHOCOLATE CHIP PROTEIN COOKIES

Classic soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies loaded with protein. These macro-verified cookies deliver 21g protein per serving while tasting like the real thing.

INGREDIENTS

  • 120g oat flour
  • 60g vanilla whey protein powder
  • 3g baking soda
  • 2g salt
  • 115g almond butter
  • 80g maple syrup
  • 1 large egg
  • 5g vanilla extract
  • 60g dark chocolate chips

NUTRITION (PER 1 COOKIE)

Calories 165 kcal
Protein 21g
Carbs 14g
Fat 6g
Fiber 2g
Sugar 5g

INSTRUCTIONS

1

Preheat and Prep

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.

2

Mix Dry Ingredients

In a large bowl, whisk together the oat flour, protein powder, baking soda, and salt until well combined.

3

Combine Wet Ingredients

In a separate bowl, mix the almond butter, maple syrup, egg, and vanilla extract until smooth.

4

Form Dough

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and stir until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips. Do not overmix.

5

Shape and Bake

Scoop 12 equal portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet. Flatten slightly with your palm. Bake for 10-12 minutes until edges are golden but centers look slightly underdone.

6

Cool

Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack. They will firm up as they cool.

PRO TIPS

  • Use a kitchen scale for precise gram measurements to ensure accurate macros.
  • Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days, or freeze for up to 3 months.
  • Don't overbake - cookies will continue to firm up as they cool on the baking sheet.
