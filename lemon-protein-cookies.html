LEMON PROTEIN COOKIES

Bright, zesty lemon cookies with 17g protein. Light and refreshing with a tender crumb and optional lemon glaze.

INGREDIENTS

  • 120g oat flour
  • 60g vanilla whey protein powder
  • 3g baking powder
  • 2g salt
  • 85g Greek yogurt
  • 60g maple syrup
  • 30g coconut oil, melted
  • 10g fresh lemon zest
  • 30ml fresh lemon juice
  • 5g vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS

1

Preheat

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2

Mix Dry

Whisk together oat flour, protein powder, baking powder, and salt.

3

Mix Wet

Combine Greek yogurt, maple syrup, coconut oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla.

4

Combine

Add wet to dry and mix until just combined.

5

Shape

Scoop 12 portions onto baking sheet. Flatten slightly.

6

Bake

Bake for 9-10 minutes until edges are just set. Do not overbake.

7

Cool and Glaze

Cool completely. Optional: drizzle with a glaze made from powdered sweetener and lemon juice.
