WHITE CHOCOLATE MACADAMIA PROTEIN COOKIES

Buttery white chocolate macadamia cookies with 18g protein. A bakery-style favorite made macro-friendly without sacrificing flavor.

INGREDIENTS

  • 120g oat flour
  • 60g vanilla whey protein powder
  • 3g baking soda
  • 2g salt
  • 85g cashew butter
  • 80g maple syrup
  • 1 large egg
  • 5g vanilla extract
  • 45g white chocolate chips
  • 40g chopped macadamia nuts

INSTRUCTIONS

1

Preheat

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2

Mix Dry

Whisk together oat flour, protein powder, baking soda, and salt.

3

Mix Wet

Combine cashew butter, maple syrup, egg, and vanilla until smooth.

4

Combine

Add wet to dry and mix. Fold in white chocolate chips and macadamia nuts.

5

Bake

Scoop 12 portions onto baking sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes until edges are golden.

6

Cool

Cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring.
