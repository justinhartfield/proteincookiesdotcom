GLUTEN-FREE PROTEIN COOKIES

Celiac-safe gluten-free protein cookies with 20g protein. Made with certified GF oats and almond flour for a safe, delicious treat.

INGREDIENTS

  • 90g certified gluten-free oat flour
  • 60g almond flour
  • 60g vanilla whey protein powder
  • 3g baking soda
  • 2g salt
  • 115g almond butter
  • 80g maple syrup
  • 1 large egg
  • 5g vanilla extract

NUTRITION (PER 1 COOKIE)

Calories 170 kcal
Protein 20g
Carbs 12g
Fat 8g
Fiber 2g
Sugar 5g

INSTRUCTIONS

1

Preheat

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2

Mix Dry

Whisk together GF oat flour, almond flour, protein powder, baking soda, and salt.

3

Mix Wet

Combine almond butter, maple syrup, egg, and vanilla until smooth.

4

Combine

Add wet to dry and mix until a dough forms.

5

Shape

Scoop 12 portions onto the baking sheet. Flatten slightly.

6

Bake

Bake for 10-12 minutes until edges are golden.

7

Cool

Cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring.

PRO TIPS

  • Use a kitchen scale for precise gram measurements to ensure accurate macros.
  • Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days, or freeze for up to 3 months.
  • Don't overbake - cookies will continue to firm up as they cool on the baking sheet.
